Data & Privacy Choices

Last updated: January 2, 2026

At HolisticMe, you have control over your data. This page explains your privacy choices and how to exercise your data rights.

🔒 Your Data Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Opt out of certain data processing activities

📦 Export Your Data

You can download a complete copy of your HolisticMe data, including:

  • Your wellness profile and constitution assessment
  • Symptom logs and daily check-ins
  • Food scan history and analysis results
  • Meal plans and saved recipes
  • GingerBee chat history

To export your data, go to Profile → Data & Privacy in the app, or contact us at the email below.

Request Data Export
Data exports are typically processed within a reasonable timeframe, generally within 7 business days. You'll receive a secure download link via email.

🗑 Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your HolisticMe account and all associated data. This action:

  • Removes all your personal information from our systems
  • Deletes your health data, scan history, and preferences
  • Cancels any active subscriptions
  • Cannot be undone

To delete your account, go to Profile → Data & Privacy → Delete Account in the app, or contact us.

Request Account Deletion
Account deletion is processed within a reasonable period, typically within 30 days. Backup systems are purged within a reasonable timeframe.

⚙ Privacy Settings

Manage your privacy preferences in the app:

  • Analytics: Control whether anonymized usage data is collected
  • Marketing: Opt in or out of promotional communications
  • Notifications: Choose which app notifications you receive

Go to Profile → Settings in the app to manage these preferences.

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

We do not sell your personal information.

European Residents (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our lawful bases for processing include:

  • Contract: To provide the services you requested
  • Consent: For marketing and optional features
  • Legitimate Interests: For security and service improvement

Contact Our Privacy Team

For any privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@holisticme.app
  • Response time: Within 30 days for formal requests

For general support inquiries, contact support@holisticme.app
